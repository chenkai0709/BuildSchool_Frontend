About Us

TAKE IT TAKEN

TAKEN is a Manufacture specialized in tuning fishing reels.

We deal in merchandise of Korean high-precision bearings and alumite treatment parts, and also manufacture various metalworking products. Based on our abundant experience of reel maintenance, we are developing custom reel parts that raise reel performance, and excite anglers.
